# Ham and Split Pea Soup

## Ingredients

- Mirepoix - Mix pre-bought from store or:
  - 1 onion, diced
  - 2 stalks celery, diced
  - 2 carrots, diced
- 1/2 tsp dried thyme
- 1 lbs diced ham
- 1 lbs dried split peas
- 6 C chicken stock
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 bay leaf
- If you're feeling inspired, 2 slices of bacon, diced

## Instructions

Toss everything in the slow cooker, and cook on low for 8 hours.
