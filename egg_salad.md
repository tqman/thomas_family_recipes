# Egg Salad

## Ingredients

- 8 large eggs
- 1/2 C kewpie mayonnaise
- 1/4 C chopped green onion
- 2 tsp dijon mustard
- 1/4 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
- salt and black pepper to taste

## Instructions

Cover 8 eggs with hot water from tap, and bring to a boil for 1 minute. Leave covered for 14 minutes, then drain the water and replace it with cold water.

Peel hard-boiled eggs and add mayonnaise, mustard, spices, and onion. Mix with a fork or the back of a spoon until the yolks of the eggs are incorporated with the other ingredients.
