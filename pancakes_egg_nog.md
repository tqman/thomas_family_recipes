# Egg Nog Pancakes

## Ingredients

- 3 C flour
- 1 Tbsp sugar
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- Â½ tsp salt
- 3 Tbsp melted butter
- 3 eggs
- 3 C Egg Nog

## Instructions

1. Combine the dry ingredients, and mix well.
2. Beat the eggs. Melt the butter. Mix the butter into the eggs. Add the egg nog to the wet ingredients.
3. Mix all the wet into the dry, stirring vigorously and for as short a period of time as possible.
