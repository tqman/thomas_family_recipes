1 small onion, liquified (with handy-chopper, or similar)
2 slices sandwich bread, torn into quarters
⅓ C whole milk
8 garlic cloves, minced
½ C (20g) grated parmesan cheese
¼ C (5g) minced fresh parsley
1 tsp dried or 1 Tbsp fresh oregano, minced
¾ tsp salt
¼ tsp red pepper
2 large eggs
1¼ lbs 85% lean ground beef
4 oz Italian sausage

Preheat an oven to 350 degrees Farehnheit.

Liquify the onion. Place the sandwich bread, milk, and onion liquid in
a medium bowl and mash with a fork until combined. Combine all other
ingredients in bowl. Roll about 16 1½" meatballs from this mixture,
and place on a rack on a rimmed baking sheet (fat will render off). Bake
at 350F for 30 minutes.

Pasta is 50g dry / person
