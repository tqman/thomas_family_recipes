# Cheeseburger Macaroni "Hamburger Helper"

## Ingredients

- 1 lbs ground beef
- 1 cup water
- 2 cups milk
- 1 1/2 cups elbow macaroni
- 226 grams (2 C; 8 oz by weight) shredded cheddar cheese
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 onion, chopped
- 1 Tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp sugar

## Instructions

Brown ground beef in large skillet. Drain and remove beef, wipe out pan.

Mix the water, cornstarch, sugar and paprika in a bowl and stir to combine.

Put a little oil in the skillet, and sautee the onion until soft and translucent, about 8 minutes. Add garlic and stir until fragrant, about 30 seconds.

Add water mixture and milk. Bring to a boil.

Add pasta, cover, reduce heat and simmer for $PASTA_COOK_TIME. Remove from heat, add cheese and beef, stir.

Let stand 5 minutes; sauce will thicken.

Top with additional cheese if you want, and salt and pepper to taste.
