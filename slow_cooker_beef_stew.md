# Slow Cooker Beef Stew

## Ingredients

- 3 onions, chopped
- ¼ C tomato paste
- 6 garlic cloves, chopped
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- ⅓ C all-purpose flour
- 3 C braising liquid (see below)
- ⅓ C soy sauce
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 lbs beef stew meat
- 1 lbs carrots, peeled, and sliced 1" thick
- 12 small potatoes
- ½ tsp salt (may need more)
- Pepper
- Oil for cooking

## Instructions

Heat oil in skillet. Add onions, garlic, and tomato paste. Sauté until onions are brown, 8-10 minutes. Add flour, and cook for one minute. Deglaze with ½ the braising liquid, whisking to remove any flour lumps.

Dump everything in the slow cooker and cook for 9-11 hours on low or 5-7 hours on high.

Let settle for five minutes and skim off any fat; serve.

## Braising liquid suggestions

- 1½ C chicken broth
- 1½ C beef broth
- Pinot Noir
