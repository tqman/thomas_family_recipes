# Dill Cucumber Salad

## Ingredients

- 1 English cucumber (340 g), unpeeled, very thinly sliced
- 1/8 C (5g) finely chopped fresh dill
- 1/4 C distilled white vinegar
- 1 tsp coarse kosher salt
- 1 Tbsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

Toss it all together and refrigerate at least fifteen minutes.
