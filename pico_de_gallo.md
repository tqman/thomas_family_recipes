# Pico de Gallo

## Ingredients

- 1½ lbs ripe tomatoes, cut into ¼- to ½-inch dice (about 3 C)
- Kosher salt
- ½ large white onion, finely diced
- 1 to 2 serrano or jalapeño chiles, finely diced (seeds and membranes removed for a milder salsa)
- ½ C finely chopped fresh cilantro leaves
- 1 Tbsp (15 ml) lime juice from 1 lime

## Instructions

1. Season tomatoes with 1 tsp (4g) salt and toss to combine.
2. Transfer to a fine-mesh strainer or colander set in a bowl and allow to drain for 20 to 30 minutes. Discard liquid.
3. Combine drained tomatoes with onion, chiles, cilantro, and lime juice.
