# Bloody Mary

## Ingredients

- 3 oz vodka
- 6 oz tomato juice
- Juice of one lemon
- Sriracha sauce (to taste, start with a few dashes)
- Salt (to taste)
- 1/2 tsp fish sauce
- 1/2 tsp celery salt
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- Optional garnishes: celery stalk, lemon wedge, olives, pickled vegetables

## Instructions

Fill a pint glass or highball glass with ice.

Add the vodka, tomato juice, lemon juice, fish sauce, celery salt, and Worcestershire sauce to the glass.

Add Sriracha to taste (start with 3-4 dashes and adjust).

Stir well with a bar spoon or long spoon to combine and chill, about 15-20 seconds.

Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and additional Sriracha if desired.

Garnish with celery stalk, lemon wedge, and/or other garnishes as desired.

Serve immediately.
