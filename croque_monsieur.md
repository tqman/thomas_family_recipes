# Croque Monsieur

## Ingredients

- 50g sourdough bread - 120 kcal
- 12g gruyere cheese - 60 kcal
- 1 slice prosciutto - 40 kcal
- Dijon mustard
- Butter

## Instructions

Cook as you would a grilled cheese. Heat a griddle over medium heat, and melt butter on it. Cook on one side for 3 - 5 minutes or until golden brown, and flip. Cook until golden brown on the other side, and serve.
