# Pecan Pie Bars

## Ingredients

### Crust:
- 1 pkg (18.5 oz) yellow cake mix, ⅔ C reserved
- ½ C butter or margarine, melted
- 1 egg

### Filling:
- ⅔ C reserved cake mix
- ½ C brown sugar
- 1½ C dark Karo syrup
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 3 eggs
- 1 C coarsely cut pecans

## Instructions

1. Grease 13 x 9 in. cake pan. Reserve ⅔ C cake mix for filling.
2. Mix first 3 ingredients (remaining cake mix, melted butter, 1 egg). Press into prepared pan.
3. Bake at 350 degrees F for 15-20 minutes until light brown.
4. Meanwhile prepare filling from reserved ⅔ C cake mix, brown sugar, Karo syrup, vanilla, and 3 eggs.
5. Pour filling over partially baked crust and sprinkle with pecans.
6. Return to oven for 30 - 35 minutes until well set.
7. Cool. Cut into bars or squares.

Makes about 4 dozen.

## Notes
