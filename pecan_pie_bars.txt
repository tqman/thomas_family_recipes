1 pkg (18.5 oz) yellow cake mix, ⅔ C reserved
½ C butter or margarine, melted
1 egg
½ C brown sugar
1½ C dark Karo syrup
1 tsp vanilla
3 eggs
1 C coarsely cut pecans

Grease 13 x 9 in. cake pan.Reserve ⅔ C cake mix for filling. Mix
first 3 ingredients. Press into prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees F
for 15-20 minutes until light brown.

Meanwhile prepare filling from next 5 ingredients. Pour over partially
baked crust und sprinkle with pecans. Return to oven for 30 - 35 minutes
until well set. Cool. Cut into bars or squares.

Makes about 4 dozen.

Note: This was Grandmother Ethel's "secret" recipe
