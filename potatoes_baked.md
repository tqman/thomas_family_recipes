# Baked Potatoes

## Ingredients

- Russet potatoes
- Melted butter
- Kosher salt
- Butter, sour cream, and fresh chives for serving

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven 450F.

2. Place Russet potatoes on a foil-lined baking sheet (foil-lined for easier cleanup).

3. Stab potatoes all over with a fork so they don't explode.

4. Brush potatoes with melted butter.

5. Sprinkle kosher salt over the buttered potatoes.

6. Roast for 45 minutes.

7. Cut each potato open, and pat the ends firmly to open it up and fluff it.

8. Serve immediately with butter, sour cream, and fresh chives.
