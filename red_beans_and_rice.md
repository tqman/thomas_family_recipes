# Red Beans and Rice

## Ingredients

- 1 lbs dried red beans
- 5C or 6 C chicken stock (pressure cooker or slow cooker, respectively)
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 celery stalks, chopped
- 7 garlic cloves, mashed
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 Tbsp Creole Seasoning
- 1 sprig fresh thyme
- 6 fresh sage leaves
- ¼ lbs dice Pancetta
- ½ tsp salt

### Garnish

- Sliced scallions
- Hot white rice
- Andoille sausage, sliced lengthwise and pan-fried to heat and brown

## Instructions

Place all of it in the pressure or slow cooker. Pressure cooker, cook for 50 minutes, then natural pressure release. Slow cooker, cook on low for eight hours.

## Notes

Used 1 lbs of sausage, added in some pork shoulder, and cooked on low for eight hours.
