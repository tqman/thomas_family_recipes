# Tuna Fish Salad

One sandwich is 75g of tuna salad. This recipe yields eight sandwiches.

## Ingredients

- 1 lbs well-done tuna, flaked
- 2 stalks celery, diced
- 120 g homemade [mayonnaise](mayonnaise.md)
- 3 Tbsp Dijon mustard
- 2 tsp finely chopped dill
- ¼ medium sweet onion, finely chopped
- ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
- ½ tsp salt

## Instructions

Mix it all together in a bowl.
