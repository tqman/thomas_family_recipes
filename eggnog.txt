
6 large eggs 3/4C granulated sugar 2C whole milk 1C heavy cream 1 tsp
vanilla extract 1 cinnamon stick 1/2 tsp nutmeg, freshly grated


Places eggs in the Instant Pot insert, and blend with stick blender to
break the yolks.


Add sugar, whole milk, heavy cream, vanilla extract, and nutmeg in the
Instant Pot insert. Hit it with the stick blender until smooth, frothy,
and the sugar has dissolved, about 30 seconds.


Set the Instant Pot to cook for one hour at 144F.
