# Mango Ice Cream

## Ingredients

- 2 mangoes, peeled and roughly chopped
- ⅓ C water
- ¾ C sugar
- 1 C heavy cream

## Instructions

Mix ingredients together and pour into ice cream maker. Run ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions.
