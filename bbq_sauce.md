# BBQ Sauce

## Ingredients

- 1/2 onion, minced
- 4 cloves garlic, smashed
- 3/4 C bourbon whiskey
- 2 C ketchup
- 1/4 C tomato paste
- 1 Tbsp cider vinegar
- 1/4 C Worcestershire sauce
- 1/4 tsp tabasco sauce

## Instructions

Place onion, garlic and whiskey in a saucepan. Simmer for ten minutes. Puree with stick blender. Then, add other ingredients and simmer for 20. Cool and bottle.
