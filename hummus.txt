3 cloves garlic
1 (19 oz) can garbanzo beans, 1/2 C of the liquid reserved
Juice of one lemon
3 Tbsp tahini
1/4 tsp salt
black pepper to taste
2 Tbsp olive oil

Chop the garlic in the food processor. Then, add everything (including
the reserved liquid) except the olive oil and blend it until it's hummus.

Transfer to a bowl, and then add the olive oil on top, and stir it in.
