# Whipped Cream

## Ingredients

- 2 C (1 pint) chilled heavy cream (38% butterfat)
- 2 Tbsp baker's sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

## Instructions

Add sugar and vanilla extract to heavy cream and swirl or stir to dissolve completely. Charge with two cream chargers, and shake 5 - 10 times. Use immediately or refrigerate for up to 10 days until needed.
