# Hoppin' John

## Ingredients

- 280g dry rice (2 rice cooker cups for Zojirushi)
- 560g water
- Black-eyed peas from [black_eyed_peas.md](black_eyed_peas.md)

## Instructions

Make the rice via your preferred method (which, just saying, should be a rice cooker).

Make the black-eyed peas.

Top the rice with the black-eyed peas.
