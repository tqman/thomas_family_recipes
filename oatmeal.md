# Oatmeal

## Ingredients

- 50g oats
- 225g water
- Pinch salt
- Heaping teaspoon of brown sugar
- 1 Tbsp butter

## Instructions

In the rice cooker, combine all ingredients.

## Notes

As above, that's: 188 calories from oatmeal, 34 calories from brown sugar (assuming 2 Tbsp), 102 calories from butter

324 calories total
