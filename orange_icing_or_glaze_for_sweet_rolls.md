# Orange Icing or Glaze for Sweet Rolls

## Ingredients

- Zest of one orange
- Juice from one orange
- Â½ tsp vanilla extract
- 2 C confectioner's sugar
- Pinch salt
- 1 - 3 Tbsp milk or cream

## Instructions

Place all ingredients except the milk or cream in a bowl. Whisk together. Add milk or cream until desired consistency is achieved. Use immediately.
