# Refried Beans

## Ingredients

- Canola oil, or bacon fat
- 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- Â¼ tsp cayenne pepper
- 3 C (3 cans) drained cooked pinto beans, cooking liquid reserved

## Instructions

Sweat the onion 8-10 minutes until translucent. Add everything and cook for 3-5 minutes (until warm). Finally, mash with a potato masher or blend with immersion blender.
