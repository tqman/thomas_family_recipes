# Slow Cooker Meatballs and Marinara

## Ingredients

### Marinara Sauce

- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 2 onions, minced
- 8 garlic cloves, minced
- ¼ C tomato paste
- 2 Tbsp minced fresh oregano or 2 tsp dried
- ¼ tsp red pepper flakes
- ½ C dry red wine
- 2 28 oz cans crushed tomatoes
- ½ C water (or better: Vodka!)
- 2 Tbsp soy sauce

### Meatballs

- 2 slices sandwich bread, torn into quarters
- ⅓ C whole milk
- 1¼ lbs 85% lean ground beef
- 4 oz. Italian sausage, removed from casing
- ½ C grated parmesan cheese (1 oz), plus extra for serving
- ¼ C minced fresh parsley
- 2 large egg yolks
- Salt and Pepper

### Finishing

- 2 Tbsp chopped fresh basil
- 1 tsp sugar, plus extra as needed

## Instructions

Heat oil in 12-innch skillet over med-high heat. Add onions, tomato paste, garlic, oregano, and red pepper flakes, and cook until onions are softened and lightly browned, 8 - 10 minutes.

Transfer half of onion mixture to mixing bowl, set aside. Deglaze pan with wine, and transfer to slow cooker. Add tomatoes, water/vodka, and soy sauce to the slow cooker.

Add bread and milk to onion mixture and mash with a fork. Mix in ground beef, sausage, parmesan, parsley, egg yolks, ¾ tsp salt, and ½ tsp pepper using hands. Roll into 1½" meatballs (about 18 meatballs total)

Microwave meatballs on plate on high for 5-7 minutes, until firm and fat renders. Discard fat, place meatballs in slow cooker. Cover and cook until meatballs are tender, 4 - 6 hours on low.

Let meatballs and sauce settle for five minutes. Skim fat from surface using a large spoon. Stir in basil and sugar. Add salt, pepper and sugar to taste if needed, and serve.
