# Mango Salsa

## Ingredients

- 2 mangoes, peeled and chopped
- 2 avocados, peeled and chopped
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 2 Thai chilis, chopped
- 5 cloves garlic
- Juice of 1Â½ limes
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 Tbsp (?) olive oil

## Instructions

Combine all the ingredients in a bowl. Can serve immediately, but is improved by giving it at least two hours in the refrigerator for flavors to combine.
