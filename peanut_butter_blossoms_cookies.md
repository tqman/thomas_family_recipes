# Peanut Butter Blossoms Cookies

## Ingredients

- 48 HERSHEY'S KISSES Brand Milk Chocolates
- ½ C shortening
- ¾ C Creamy Peanut Butter
- ⅓ C granulated sugar
- ⅓ C light brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 Tbsp milk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1½ C all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- ½ tsp salt
- Additional granulated sugar

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375°F. Remove wrappers from chocolates.
2. Beat shortening and peanut butter in large bowl until well blended. Add ⅓ cup granulated sugar and brown sugar; beat until fluffy. Add egg, milk and vanilla; beat well. Stir together flour, baking soda and salt; gradually beat into peanut butter mixture.
3. Shape dough into 1-inch balls. Roll in granulated sugar; place on ungreased cookie sheet.
4. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned. Immediately press a chocolate into center of each cookie.
