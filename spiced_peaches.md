# Spiced Peaches

## Ingredients

- ½ C sugar
- ½ C water
- ¼ C apple cider vinegar
- 8 to 10 whole cloves
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 2 Tbsp brown sugar
- Canned peaches in no syrup, 28 oz

## Instructions

In a large saucepan, bring the first six ingredients to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer 10 minutes. Add peaches; simmer until heated through, about 10 minutes.

Pour into a shallow dish. Cover and chill 8 hours or overnight.
