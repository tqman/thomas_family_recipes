1½ lbs ripe tomatoes, cut into ¼- to ½-inch dice (about 3 C)
Kosher salt
½ large white onion, finely diced
1 to 2 serrano or jalapeño chiles, finely diced (seeds and membranes removed for a milder salsa)
½ C finely chopped fresh cilantro leaves
1 Tbsp (15 ml) lime juice from 1 lime

Season tomatoes with 1 tsp (4g) salt and toss to
combine. Transfer to a fine-mesh strainer or colander set in a bowl
and allow to drain for 20 to 30 minutes. Discard liquid.
