# Modernist Queso

## Ingredients

- 265g wheat beer or water (I used a bitter white ale and it was awesome)
- 11g sodium citrate
- 285g pepper jack cheese

## Instructions

Add liquid to a small saucepan and bring to a simmer. Add sodium citrate and stir to dissolve. Add cheese a handful at a time, stirring until smooth with each addition. Bring to a light simmer for five minutes. Serve warm.
