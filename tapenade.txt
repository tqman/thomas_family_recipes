2 C stuffed olives, sliced
1 shallot, minced
2 tomatoes, seeded and diced
2 Tbsp shallots, rinsed
1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar

Saute shallots until soft. Add tomatoes, shallots and olives, and cook for two
minutes, until just warm and softened. Remove from heat, and toss with
vinegar. Add salt and pepper (if needed) to taste
