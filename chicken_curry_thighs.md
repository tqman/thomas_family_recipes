# Chicken Curry Thighs

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
- 1 medium onion, sliced
- 2 cinnamon sticks
- 4 green cardamom pods
- 4-5 cloves
- 3-4 whole peppercorns
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1 tsp cumin powder
- 1 tsp cayenne powder
- 1 tsp Garam Masala
- 1 1/2 tsps salt, or to taste
- 7 cloves garlic, minced (or smashed)
- 1 Tbsp fresh tumeric, peeled and chopped (or 1 tsp dried)
- 1 Tbsp fresh gingner, peeled and chopped (or 1 tsp dried)
- 1/2 C raisins
- 13.5 oz coconut milk
- 2 Tbsp chopped fresh cilantro (optional)

## Instructions

Put everything but cilantro in the slow cooker, and cook 8 hours on low or 5 hours on high (and with this recipe it doesn't really matter what your choice is).

At the end of the cook, shred the chicken into the sauce with a spoon. Top with chopped cilantro. Serve with rice.
