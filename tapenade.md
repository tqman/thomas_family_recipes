# Tapenade

## Ingredients

- 2 C stuffed olives, sliced
- 1 shallot, minced
- 2 tomatoes, seeded and diced
- 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar

## Instructions

Saute shallots until soft. Add tomatoes, and olives, and cook for two minutes, until just warm and softened. Remove from heat, and toss with vinegar. Add salt and pepper (if needed) to taste.

Goes well on firm, white-fleshed fish.
