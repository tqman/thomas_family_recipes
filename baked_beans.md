# Baked Beans

## Ingredients

- Homemade bbq sauce
- 4 pieces of thick-cut bacon
- 1 onion, diced
- ¼ green pepper, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 25 oz can of pinto beans
- ¾ C bbq sauce
- 2 Tbsp Dijon mustard

## Instructions

Start with homemade bbq sauce.

Preheat oven to 400.

Take four pieces of thick-cut bacon, and put them in a frying pan, and cook for ten minutes at 400 in the skillet.

Remove the bacon and set it aside.

Dice one onion and ¼ of a green pepper. Saute in the pan in the grease from the bacon until onion is translucent. Add in four chopped garlic cloves, 1 25 oz can of pinto beans, ¾ C of bbq sauce and 2 Tbsp of Dijon mustard. Bring to a simmer.

Pour the bean mixture into a small baking pan, and cover with the four pieces of bacon. Bake at 325 for two hours.
