# Pickles

## Ingredients

Makes one jar worth of brine

### Brine:

- 1C Water
- 1C Vinegar
- 1½ Tbsp salt
- ½ tsp sugar

### Per pickle jar:

- 12 cloves garlic
- ½ habeñero pepper
- 1 tsp dill seeds
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 1 tsp peppercorns
- 2 sprigs fresh dill
