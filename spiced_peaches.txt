1/2 C sugar
1/2 C water
1/4 C apple cider vinegar
8 to 10 whole cloves
1 cinnamon stick
Canned peaches in no syrup, 28 oz (2 cans)
2 tablespoons brown sugar

In a large saucepan, bring the first five ingredients to a
boil. Reduce heat; simmer 10 minutes. Add peaches; simmer until heated
through, about 10 minutes.

Pour into a shallow dish. Cover and chill 8 hours or overnight.
