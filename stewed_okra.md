# Stewed Okra

## Ingredients

- 4 slices bacon
- 1 sweet onion, chopped
- 7 cloves garlic, chopped fine
- 3 C sliced okra
- 1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
- ½ tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 C chicken stock
- 1 bay leaf
- ½ tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp dried cumin
- 1 tsp salt

## Instructions

In a dutch oven, cook the bacon until crispy. Remove from pan and pat dry, and crumble.

Add the onion and sauté in the bacon fat until translucent, about seven minutes. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about thirty seconds.

Add the okra and tomatoes (including juice). Add in the crumbled bacon, cayenne, bay leaf, oregano, cumin, and salt, and simmer for 15 minutes.

If the pan becomes dry, add chicken stock.
