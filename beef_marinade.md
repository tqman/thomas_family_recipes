# Beef Marinade

## Ingredients

- 1/2 C Worcestershire sauce
- 1/4 C soy sauce
- 3 Tbsp brown sugar
- 4 anchovy filets (or 1 Tbsp fish sauce)
- 2 cloves garlic
- 2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
- 2 Tbsp tomato paste
- 1/2 C vegetable oil
- 2 Tbsp chopped chives
- 1 medium shallot, sliced
- 1 whole flank steak, about 2 pounds (see note)

## Instructions

Put it all in the blender cup, hit with stick blender.

Reserve 1/3C to serve as sauce with steak, and put steak in a vacuum bag with the marinade and seal it. Let it marinade for at least an hour (or sous vide with it).
