# Key Lime Cake

## Ingredients

### Key Lime Cake

- 1 C Neutral Oil
- 3 large Eggs
- 1⅓ C Whole Milk
- 2 Tbsp Fresh Lime Zest
- 1 Tbsp Fresh Lime Juice
- 2 C (396g) Sugar
- 2 C (240g) Flour
- 1 tsp Baking Powder
- ½ tsp Baking Soda
- 1 tsp Salt

### Lime Cream Cheese Frosting

- ½ C (1 stick) unsalted butter (softened)
- 8 oz Cream Cheese (softened)
- 4 - 4½ C Powdered Sugar
- 1 Tbsp Fresh Lime Juice
- ½ tsp Fresh Lime Zest
- Fresh Limes (sliced for garnish if you can be arsed)

## Instructions

Preheat oven to 325 degrees.

In a large mixing bowl, add oil, eggs, whole milk, lime zest, and lime juice. Cream for 2 minutes.

In a large bowl, add sugar, flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Stir into egg mixture and fold.

Spray one 9x13", two 9", or three 8" cake pans with nonstick cooking spray. Place batter into baking pans. Cooking times:

- 1 9x13": 37 minutes
- 2 9": 30-36 minutes
- 3 8": 20-26 minutes

Turn out onto a wire rack and cool completely before applying frosting.

### To make Lime Cream Cheese Frosting

In a large bowl, cream together butter and cream cheese for 4-5 minutes until light and fluffy, scraping sides of bowl often. Add powdered sugar, lime juice, lime zest, and cream together. Chill.

If baked in 1 9x13" pan, cut cooled cake in half to make two layers.

Spread frosting on top of one cooled cake layer. Place the other cooled cake layer on top and spread frosting all over. Garnish with fresh lime slices.
