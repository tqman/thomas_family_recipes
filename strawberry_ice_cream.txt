2 C heavy cream
1 C whole milk
1 C cup (200g) sugar
2 eggs
½ tsp vanilla
400g fresh fruit, pureed
¼ tsp salt
1 Tbsp fresh lemon juice

Puree strawberries

Beat the eggs

Add to the eggs milk, cream, sugar and vanilla, and finally strawberries

Pour mixture in the machine and let run for 25 minutes

Freeze for four hours to fully harden
