# Pumpkin Ice Cream

## Ingredients

- 1 C heavy whipping cream
- ½ C whole milk
- 1½ C (1 15 oz can) canned pumpkin
- 1 C packed brown sugar
- 1 tsp ground ginger
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- ¼ tsp salt
- ⅛ tsp ground cloves

## Instructions

Mix it all up and put it in the ice cream maker
